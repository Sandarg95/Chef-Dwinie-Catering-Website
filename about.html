Our Story

Founded in the vibrant city of Indianapolis, Chef Dwinie Catering has its roots deeply embedded in the rich culture of the Haitian community. Our journey began with a passion to share the authentic tastes of the Caribbean and specifically, the soulful flavors of Haiti, with the diverse and welcoming people of Indiana.

At Chef Dwinie Catering, we believe that food is a powerful bridge that connects people, transcending borders and bringing communities together. Our commitment is to serve not just delicious meals but also to create a memorable culinary experience that reflects the warmth and hospitality of our Haitian heritage.

Our Mission

Our mission is clear - to bring the vibrant and delectable flavors of Caribbean and Haitian cuisine to the tables of Indiana. We take pride in sourcing high-quality ingredients, infusing each dish with the love and care that defines home-cooked meals. Whether you are a part of the Haitian community or an adventurous food enthusiast, our goal is to transport you to the tropical paradise of Haiti through every bite.

Join us on this culinary journey as we celebrate diversity, embrace community, and savor the extraordinary flavors that make Chef Dwinie Catering a unique and cherished part of Indiana's gastronomic landscape.
